This package contains 12 different types of airliners and helicopters.
All Objects including 2 different Texture-Sets and Wheels are animateable.

Just drag the prefabs into your scene and arrange them as you like.

You may also want to have a look at the example scene that contains all the prefabs.

Textures-sizes between 512x512 to 2048x2048.
Textures are created using Ambient-Occlusion.
Polygon count per object between 5.000 and 60.000.

The original FBX-files are also included in the package.
